How to Configure Your Custom Domain

  1. Log in to your domain registrar's website.
  2. Navigate to the DNS Management or DNS Settings section.
  3. Add a new CNAME record with the following details:
    • Name/Host: www (This is the subdomain you'll be pointing to your funnel. If you want to point the root domain, use @ instead.)
    • Value/Points to: 13.36.20.246 (This is your server's IP address.)
    • TTL: 3600 (or 1 hour) â€“ You can leave this as the default or lower it for quicker propagation.
  4. Save your changes.
  5. For enhanced security and performance, it's recommended to connect your domain through Cloudflare:
    1. Sign up for a free Cloudflare account at cloudflare.com.
    2. Add your domain to Cloudflare by following the on-screen instructions.
    3. Once your domain is added, navigate to the DNS settings within Cloudflare.
    4. Add a CNAME record with the following details:
      • Name/Host: www
      • Value/Points to: yourdomain.com (or the server's IP).
      • Ensure that Cloudflare's proxy is enabled (orange cloud icon) for SSL and CDN benefits.
    5. Update the nameservers for your domain registrar to the ones provided by Cloudflare (this step will appear after adding your domain).
    6. Wait for DNS propagation (can take up to 48 hours).
  6. Ensure SSL is enabled in Cloudflare to secure your site. For most setups, you can use Flexible SSL (no server-side certificate needed), but Full SSL is recommended if your server has a valid certificate.
  7. Once DNS propagation is complete, your custom domain will point to your funnel landing page.
  8. Clear your browser cache and verify your domain is working correctly by typing your custom domain in the browser.

Note: DNS changes can take up to 48 hours to fully propagate across the internet, but typically this process is much faster.
