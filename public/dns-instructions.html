How to Configure Your Custom Domain

  1. Log in to your domain registrar's website.
  2. Navigate to the DNS management section.
  3. Add a new CNAME record with the following details:
    • Name/Host: www
    • Value/Points to: [Your App's Domain] (e.g., your-app.herokuapp.com)
    • TTL: 3600 (or 1 hour)
  4. Save your changes.
  5. Wait for DNS propagation (can take up to 48 hours).

Once configured, your custom domain will point to your funnel landing page.
